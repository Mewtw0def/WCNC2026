The proportion of malicious traffic in the device export traffic is counted at a daily sampling rate. This file records the changes in the proportion of malicious traffic per hour for 50 UEs within 20,000 hours. There are multiple virus transmission relationships between these 50 UEs. Among the two connected UEs, the infected person has a certain probability of transmitting the virus to the susceptible and the recovered every hour, and has a certain probability of recovering to the recovered person. The number of attack packets of the infected person increases over time, and the number of attack packets of the recovered person decreases over time. The infection and recovery of each virus are independent events.
